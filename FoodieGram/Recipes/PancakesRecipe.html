Pancakes with Maple Syrup

Description

Fluffy pancakes served with pure maple syrup, a perfect breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in eggs and milk until smooth batter forms.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with pure maple syrup on top.
